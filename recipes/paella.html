Paella

Description

Whip up this easy version of the traditional Spanish seafood dish straight from the storecupboard. Add extras such as chorizo and peas if you like

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp each hot smoked paprika and dried thyme
  • 300g paella
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes with garlic
  • 900ml chicken stock

Steps

  1. Heat the oilve oil in a large frying pan. Add onion and soften for 5 minutes
  2. Add smoked paprika, thyme and paella rice, stir for 1 min, then splash in sherry
  3. Season and cook, uncovered, for about 15 minutes
  4. Stir in seafood mix and cover with a lid. Simmer for 5 mins
